📋 Your US client may ask you for Form W-8BEN-E
This IRS form confirms your Canadian corporation and claims exemption from US withholding tax under the Canada-US Tax Treaty. You fill it once per client, they keep it on file. Not filed with CRA or IRS.
📥 Download W-8BEN-E (IRS)  ·  Instructions
