Frequently asked questions

Do I need accounting experience?
No. TradeBooks is built for business owners, not accountants. The AI handles categorization, HST extraction, and tax calculations. You just review what it did and confirm. If something looks wrong, change it with a dropdown — the AI learns your patterns over time.
Which banks are supported?
All major Canadian banks — BMO, TD, RBC, Scotiabank, CIBC, Desjardins — plus credit cards from AMEX, Visa, Mastercard, and Capital One. Upload any PDF or CSV statement.
Is my financial data secure?
Your data lives in your own Google Sheet, in your own Google account. We don't store your transactions, receipts, or financial records. The AI processes your data in real-time and never retains it. Your Google Sheet is as secure as your Google account.
Can I use this for my corporation AND sole proprietorship?
Yes. During setup, you choose your business type — sole proprietor or incorporated. TradeBooks shows the right tax forms for each: T2125 for sole props, T2 for corporations. Both get full HST, payroll, and AI tax advice.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep everything. Your Google Sheet with all your books stays in your Google Drive forever. You just lose access to the AI features (statement import, receipt scanning, tax advisor). Your data is never deleted or locked behind a paywall.
Does this replace my accountant?
For day-to-day bookkeeping, yes. TradeBooks handles statement imports, categorization, HST tracking, invoicing, and tax estimation. You may still want an accountant for year-end filing, complex tax planning, or audit situations — but you'll save thousands on routine bookkeeping.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. TradeBooks is a web app that works on any device — phone, tablet, laptop. No app to install. The receipt scanner opens your phone camera directly. Most users do their monthly bookkeeping on their phone in under 20 minutes.
